Summary of the Day

#

The main picture today is that the frontier race is shifting to the axis of agent security, long-term memory, tool-use efficiency and compute dominance rather than a new model announcement. In the April 24 unique set, MCP attack surface, cross-session threats, alignment faking, structured memory and small but industrial agent models stand out; This angle is noticeably different from the GPT-5.5 / Office agent mode / Mythos discussions of the last three days. Sources: https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.20994 | https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.21131 | https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.20995 | https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.21748 | https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.21590

The second axis is infrastructure and investment blockade. Google's up to $40 billion cash+compute investment in Anthropic, Meta's shift to millions of AWS AI CPUs / hundreds of thousands of Graviton pipelines, and DeepSeek V4's long context + low inference cost claim show that the market is shifting from GPU-centric single line to CPU+custom silicon+efficient open model combination. Sources: https://techcrunch.com/2026/04/24/google-to-invest-up-to-40b-in-anthropic-in-cash-and-compute/ | https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/24/meta-will-use-hundreds-of-thousands-of-aws-graviton-chips.html | https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/24/amazon-custom-chips-get-a-boost-from-meta-giving-the-cloud-giant-another-path-to-win-in-ai.html | https://huggingface.co/blog/deepseekv4 | https://www.theverge.com/ai-artificial-intelligence/918035/deepseek-preview-v4-ai-model

The third axis is regulation and identity / trust verification pressure. When the UK digital services tax tension, age verification-ID checkpoint debate and automatic compliance detection research are read together, not only model security but also the identification of the internet using AI and the machine implementation of regulation become the main frontier in 2026. Sources: https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/24/trump-tariffs-uk-tech-apple-google-meta.html | https://go.theregister.com/feed/www.theregister.com/2026/04/23/proton_ceo_age_checks_id_checkpoint/ | https://go.theregister.com/feed/www.theregister.com/2026/04/24/digital_it_consult/ | https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.21469
