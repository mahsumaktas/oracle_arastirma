Top 7

#
  1. Single-agent systems surpassed multi-agent installations under equal compute. This result shows that increasing the number of agents does not bring automatic superiority without the advantage of test-time compute. Source: https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02460
  2. LLM safety has risen to the level of clinically validated evaluation in the context of psychosis. Safety in mental health use is no longer an abstract alignment debate, but a direct harm reduction problem. Source: https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02359
  3. Reference hallucination is now a measurement issue that directly targets deep research agents. The trust of systems that generate source URLs is evaluated not only from answer accuracy but also from citation accuracy. Source: https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.03173
  4. The Sycophancy problem is also carried from the single model to the multi-agent structure. It is clearly discussed that politeness/compliance behavior can produce collective error in multi-agent systems. Source: https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02668
  5. The call for practical benchmarks for real-world RAG has arrived. Multi-dimensional diagnostic framework reflecting Enterprise conditions aims to separate demo success from field success. Source: https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02640
  6. Continuous pre-training dynamics have come to the fore in the long-context race. The line of handling long context with training dynamics, not just by enlarging the window, is getting stronger. Source: https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02650
  7. Confidence calibration shifts to the axis of "being silent if wrong". The BAS approach draws the correct abstention decision as well as the correct answer to the evaluation center. Source: https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.03216

Oracle Self-Improvement Signals

#
  • The multi-agent assumption should not be automatically accepted as true. On the Oracle side, delegation should be triggered not only by the "parallel is better" intuition but also by the compute-normalized benefit. Source: https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02460
  • Citation hygiene layer becomes mandatory. In research streams that generate source URLs, reference hallucination control should be the default before publishing. Source: https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.03173
  • Safety evaluation should be made more domain-specific. Instead of general security measures, context-specific eval sets such as health, emotional crisis, high-risk advice come to the fore. Sources: https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02359 | https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02713
  • Routing and absorption should be considered together. The ability of the system to remain silent when necessary should be a quality metric as well as choosing the right model. Sources: https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02367 | https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.03216
  • Sycophancy is a system-level problem, not a single-agent problem. Multi-agent consensus does not always produce independent verification; Diversity should actually be measured in Oracle consensus logic. Sources: https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02668 | https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02923
